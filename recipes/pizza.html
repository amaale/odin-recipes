Pizza

Description

Classic homemade pizza with a crispy crust, tangy tomato sauce, gooey mozzarella cheese, and your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Sliced bell peppers
  • Sliced onions
  • Chopped mushrooms
  • Olive oil
  • Basil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°. Place a pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven to preheat as well.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a lightly floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a piece of parchment paper dusted with cornmeal.
  4. Spoon tomato sauce onto the dough and spread evenly, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Top with bell peppers, onions, basil and mushrooms.
  7. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt, and pepper.
  8. Transfer the pizza (on the parchment paper) to the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven.
  9. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and melted.
  10. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
