Pasta

Description

Delicious pasta with tomato sauce, topped with Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Optional: Fresh basil leaves
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. Cook pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain.
  3. Heat pasta sauce in a separate saucepan until warmed through.
  4. Combine pasta and sauce in a large bowl, tossing to coat.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves.
