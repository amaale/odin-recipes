Brownies

Description

Decadent chocolate brownies with a fudgy texture and a rich chocolate flavor.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Granulated sugar
  • Cocoa powder
  • Eggs
  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking powder
  • Salt
  • Vanilla extract
  • Optional: Chopped nuts or chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a baking pan.
  2. Melt butter in a saucepan over low heat. Remove from heat and stir in sugar, cocoa powder, eggs, flour, baking powder, salt, and vanilla extract.
  3. If using, fold in chopped nuts or chocolate chips.
  4. Pour batter into prepared pan and spread evenly.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out with moist crumbs, not wet batter.
  6. Cool brownies in the pan on a wire rack before cutting into squares and serving.
